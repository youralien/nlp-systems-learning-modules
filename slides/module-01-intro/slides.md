# Introduction to NLP Systems

Module 1

---

## What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language.

- Understanding text and speech
- Generating human-like responses
- Translating between languages

---

## Key Concepts

1. **Tokenization** - Breaking text into smaller units
2. **Embeddings** - Representing words as vectors
3. **Attention** - Focusing on relevant parts of input
4. **Transformers** - Modern architecture for NLP

---

## Why NLP Matters

- Powering search engines
- Enabling chatbots and virtual assistants
- Automating document processing
- Breaking language barriers
